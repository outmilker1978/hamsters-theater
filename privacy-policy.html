Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What data we collect

  • Username — you choose it yourself, it is visible to other users in the room
  • Camera & microphone — used only during an active call, never recorded or stored
  • Chat messages — sent to other participants in real-time, not stored on the server
  • Device info — browser, OS, IP address (used for WebRTC connection, not logged)
  • Room ID — a temporary identifier for the room you join

What we do NOT collect

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Payment information

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Video and audio streams are encrypted (DTLS-SRTP) and go directly between participants — our server never sees or records them.

Third-party services

  • Render.com — cloud server for signaling (room creation, WebRTC handshake). No media passes through.
  • Google STUN — used for peer-to-peer connection setup.
  • Unsplash — background images may be loaded from their CDN.
